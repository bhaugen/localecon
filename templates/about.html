About this site

  • It's just getting started.
  • The goal is stated in the tagline: Communities, Clusters, Networks and Resources: Analysis, Planning, Formation, Administration and Accounting.
  • It's being developed by the people at Group Accounting, in collaboration with invited communities who are working on local economic development.
  • The plan is to start with resource flow models of local economic clusters, then add a lot of features for economic analysis. So far, that means radial graphs and function-resource tables. Lots more to come.
  • You can read more about the theory behind these models here and here.
  • After analysis, we want to assist in planning and formation of local economic networks. For example, we want to provide features to help grant writers develop proposals and business plans.
  • Then we plan to provide software for administering local economic networks. We've already done a lot of that: see software for food networks.
  • If you want to participate, or have questions, please please send us an email.
